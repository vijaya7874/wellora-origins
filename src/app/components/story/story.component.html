Our story

We grew up in the fields of Guntur, where farming is a way of life, not a job. Wellora Origins began as a way to bring those same authentic superfoods — grown honestly, processed gently — into modern kitchens across India.

Every jar names a single plant, and then it stops. Nothing added, because there is nothing we would want to add.
